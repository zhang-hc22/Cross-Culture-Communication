# Conclusion
How do movies facilitate Cross-Culture Communication?

<div grid="~ cols-2 gap-2" >
<div v-click>

- _Zootopia_:
  - Use story to express core culture values
  - Use <span v-mark.circle.red="3">symbols</span> or anchors to attract global audience and give them familiarness

</div>

<div v-click>

- _Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon_:
  - Show <span v-mark.circle.red="3">Wuxia</span> culture through characteristics
  - Express deepest feelings through specific scenes

</div>
</div>

<div v-click>

- Problem: symbolized and superficial culture impression → _Chinese culture = Kongfu?_

</div>

<div v-click>

- Future: Use emotions and common senses to draw true interest of a foreign culture
